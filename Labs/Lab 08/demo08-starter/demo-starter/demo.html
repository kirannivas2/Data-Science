Hello world. This is a first paragraph.

Paragraphs do not require us to write text all on one line. In fact they will let us write on multiple lines, putting single spaces in and breaking lines up as needed. We can use the <br> tag to
break lines up explicitly within a paragraph.

We often use escape characters for things like less than &lt; or &gt; for greater than. The &amp; stands ampersand.
